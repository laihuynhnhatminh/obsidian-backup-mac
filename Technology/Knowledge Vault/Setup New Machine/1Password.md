#password_management #configurations 
## Download and Install on desktop:

- Go to [https://1password.com/downloads/](https://1password.com/downloads/) . Download and install.
- Sign in to 1password account using the secret from phone. (otherwise from usb)

## Setting up on Browser:

- Go to https://1password.com/downloads/browser-extension. Follow the instruction and setup for browser
- Sign in to 1password on browser to finish the setup and test it.
